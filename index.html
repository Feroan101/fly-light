Coaches

Lokeshmaran

A former national player, Lokeshmaran focuses on strong fundamentals and mental toughness for every player. He believes in personalized attention and structured progression, blending traditional coaching wisdom with modern sports science to help students reach their full potential.

His sessions balance technical drills, physical conditioning, and tactical awareness to create complete players. This structured yet adaptable approach helps students build confidence, discipline, and a strong competitive mindset on and off the court.

Kirthi vasan

An NIS certified coach, Kirthi emphasizes fun-based learning for younger students while maintaining technical precision. He creates engaging drills that develop both skills and passion for the game, making training enjoyable without compromising on quality.

His patient and encouraging approach helps students build confidence alongside their badminton abilities. By combining structured practice with a positive environment, he ensures that players progress steadily while continuing to love the sport.
