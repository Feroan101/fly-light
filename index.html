About Our Academy

fly Light - The School of Badminton is dedicated to nurturing champions through expert coaching, state-of-the-art facilities, and a passion for excellence. Our experienced coaches bring years of competitive experience and are committed to developing both technical skills and mental strength in every player.

We believe in creating a supportive environment where players of all levels can thrive, from beginners taking their first steps on the court to advanced athletes preparing for competitive tournaments.
